hello. we're SANCHO

born out of a love for the delta blues, Sancho brings an electrified take on the genre in classic Detroit style. We started as a duo (guitar and drums), but after bumming around in Detroit and Hamtramck for a year, decided to transform into a power trio. We play a special blend of country blues and rock n roll with one goal in mind. GET THE PEOPLE MOVIN.

Caleb - Guitar/Vox, Spencer - Drums, Ryan - Bass
